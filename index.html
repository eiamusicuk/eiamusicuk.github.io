EiA

Artist, actor, model

An ‘ethereal powerhouse’ whose theatrical roots and mixed influences spell her unique sound.

Eleanor Roberts, known professionally as EiA, is an up and coming new artist who is paving new pathways in her music. With a clear vision, humility and grace, this cosmic butterfly is transforming the creative space around her.

Her classical training, and formative years singing opera and gospel, have transformed into an eclectic writing style. Influenced by an array of music, from the likes of Joni Mitchell and Supertramp, to more current influences like Jamie T and Florence & Machine, EiA continues to move through the musical landscape carving out her own space in the Indie dream & pop realm.

No stranger to the stage, the artist has been performing and publicly sharing her music since she was 12 years old. EiA was exposed to a whole array of life and music experiences growing up. Seven siblings and a large musical family meant a rich musical upbringing, something that has subsequently become a driving force behind her emotionally charged writing.

With an intuitive connection to the earth and a sensitivity to the hearts of everyone, she has the ability to transform people when she sings with moments of transcendental bliss. Her journey is deeply relatable, her music is of universal recognition and her message is of the struggles we know too well.

EiA is just beginning to blossom as an artist. In 2020 EiA softly released her debut single and music video ‘Orange’ and in 2021 EiA released her second single, ‘My Man’. The artist’s debut EP ‘Till I See Black’ is set for release in November 2022 and pre-mix of the EP's lead single ‘Let's Go Again’ can be heard in the Soundcloud link above.

The multi-disciplinary artist has also found success in acting, having just been cast in an Amazon Studios feature film, as well in modelling through her work with fashion photographer Jamal Anthony.
